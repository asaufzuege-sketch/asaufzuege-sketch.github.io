SmartHockey Pro – Professional Hockey Statistics App

SmartHockey Pro is the ultimate professional solution for ambitious ice hockey teams, coaches, club managers, and scouts. Whether you manage a professional team, a junior team, or an amateur squad – SmartHockey Pro gives you all the tools you need to track, analyze, and improve your team's performance at the highest level.

Key Features

📊 Player Statistics Tracking

Track every aspect of your players' performance including goals, assists, plus/minus ratings, penalty minutes, shots on goal, face-off percentages, and much more. Build a complete statistical profile for each player over the entire season.

🎯 Goal Map Visualization

Visualize where your team scores and concedes goals using our interactive goal map. Identify patterns in your offensive and defensive play, and use this data to adjust your tactical approach and improve your team's performance.

📅 Season Overview & Analytics

Get a comprehensive overview of your entire season at a glance. Track win/loss records, goal differences, points standings, and performance trends over time. Identify when your team performs best and when improvements are needed.

📤 Export Functions (CSV/PDF)

Export your statistics and reports in professional CSV and PDF formats. Share detailed reports with players, parents, club management, or scouts. Professional-quality reports that present your team's data in a clear and compelling way.

👥 Team Management with Multiple Teams

Manage multiple teams within a single app. Perfect for coaches who work with several teams simultaneously, or for clubs that want to manage all their teams from one platform. Keep everything organized and easily switch between teams.

🔄 Line-Up Management

Organize and manage your team's lines and defensive pairings. Plan your line combinations, track line performance, and optimize your rotations based on statistical data. Make informed decisions about which lines perform best in different situations.

How SmartHockey Pro Works

Getting started with SmartHockey Pro is straightforward. First, set up your team by entering your players' names, positions, and jersey numbers. You can create multiple teams within the app, making it perfect for coaches who manage several squads.

During or after games, you enter game data including goals, assists, penalties, shots, and other statistics. The app processes this information and immediately generates comprehensive statistics and visualizations. The goal map feature lets you tap on the ice surface to mark where each goal was scored, building a visual picture of your team's offensive and defensive patterns.

Between games, you can review detailed reports, compare player statistics, and analyze trends for planning. When you need to share information, the export function creates professional PDF reports or CSV files that can be opened in spreadsheet software like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.

The season overview feature gives you a bird's-eye view of your entire campaign, showing how your team has progressed, which games were decisive, and where improvements have been made. This long-term view is invaluable for planning and for annual performance reviews.

Who Is SmartHockey Pro For?

SmartHockey Pro is designed for serious hockey people who want more than just basic scorekeeping. It's ideal for coaches at all levels who want to base their decisions on data rather than gut feeling alone. It's perfect for team managers who need to maintain detailed records for club administration. It's also an excellent tool for analytics-minded assistant coaches who want to contribute detailed statistical analysis to the coaching staff.

Youth coaches will find SmartHockey Pro particularly valuable for tracking player development over multiple seasons. Parents and players can see concrete evidence of improvement in statistical form, which is highly motivating for young athletes.
