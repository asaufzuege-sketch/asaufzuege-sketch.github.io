Privacy Policy

SmartHockey Products – smarthockeyproducts.com

Last updated: April 2025

Summary: SmartHockey Products is committed to your privacy. Our core application, the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro, stores all game data locally in your own browser — we do not collect, transmit, or store your coaching data on any server. This Privacy Policy explains in detail what information is collected through our website and how it is used.

1. Who We Are

SmartHockey Products operates the website smarthockeyproducts.com and its associated web applications, including the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro. References to "we", "us", or "SmartHockey Products" in this Privacy Policy refer to the operators of these services.

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us at: support@smarthockeyproducts.com

2. Data We Collect and Why

2.1 Application Data (Game Statistics)

The SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro stores all data you enter — including team names, player rosters, game statistics, and settings — exclusively in your browser's local storage (IndexedDB and/or LocalStorage). This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to SmartHockey Products servers or any third party. We have no access to your coaching data whatsoever.

As a consequence, you are fully responsible for the safekeeping of your data. We strongly recommend using the app's built-in export functions to regularly back up your statistics to your own device.

2.2 Website Visits – Server Log Files

When you visit our website, your browser automatically transmits certain information to our web host (GitHub Pages). This may include:

  • Your IP address (anonymised by GitHub's infrastructure)
  • Date and time of the request
  • URL of the page requested
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL

This data is processed by GitHub Pages to deliver the website content to you and to ensure the technical stability and security of the hosting infrastructure. We do not have direct access to raw server logs. GitHub's privacy practices are described in the GitHub Privacy Statement.

2.3 Contact via Email

If you contact us by email, we will store and process the personal data you provide (such as your name and email address) solely for the purpose of handling your enquiry and providing you with a response. We will not use your contact details for marketing purposes without your explicit consent. Contact data is deleted once the enquiry has been resolved and there is no legal obligation to retain it.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising Cookies

Our website uses Google AdSense, an advertising service provided by Google LLC. Google AdSense uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to display personalised advertisements based on your past visits to this and other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing behaviour. The information generated by these cookies (including your IP address, truncated to preserve anonymity where required) is transmitted to and stored by Google on servers that may be located outside your country.

You have the following options to manage advertising preferences:

  • Opt out of personalised advertising via Google's Ads Settings
  • Opt out of third-party advertising cookies via aboutads.info (USA) or youronlinechoices.eu (EU)
  • Configure your browser to block cookies entirely (note: this may affect website functionality)

Google's privacy practices are described in the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Google Play and In-App Purchases

The SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro is available as an Android app through the Google Play Store. In-app purchases within the Android app are handled entirely by Google Play Billing. SmartHockey Products does not collect, process, or store any payment information (credit card details, billing addresses, etc.). All payment data is processed by Google according to their Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

Our website uses the following types of cookies:

  • Functional cookies: Used by the SmartHockey web application to remember your language preference (stored in LocalStorage). These are strictly necessary for the application to function correctly.
  • Advertising cookies: Set by Google AdSense to enable personalised advertising as described in Section 3 above.
  • Analytics cookies: We do not currently use a separate analytics service beyond what Google AdSense provides.

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impair the functionality of the web application.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR and Applicable Law

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights with respect to any personal data we hold about you:

  • Right of access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to restriction: You may request that we restrict the processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your personal data for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

Please note that since the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro stores no user data on our servers, most of the above rights are practically satisfied by the app's local-only storage model. For any personal data relating to email correspondence, please contact us at support@smarthockeyproducts.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection supervisory authority if you believe your rights have been violated.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@smarthockeyproducts.com so we can take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

Personal data collected through email contact is retained only for as long as necessary to resolve the enquiry and comply with any applicable legal retention obligations. Data processed by third-party services (Google AdSense, GitHub Pages) is subject to the retention policies of those respective services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. The date of the most recent revision is shown at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Your continued use of the Service after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.
