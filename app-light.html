SmartHockey Light – Smart Hockey Statistics for Every Player

SmartHockey Light is the perfect entry-level solution for youth teams, individual players, and amateur teams who want to track their hockey performance without being overwhelmed by complexity. With an intuitive user interface and all the essential features, SmartHockey Light makes tracking statistics accessible to everyone.

Whether you're a team manager, a youth coach for younger players, or a recreational player who wants to keep track of personal performance throughout the season and needs a simple and effective tool for team statistics – SmartHockey Light has everything you need to start your journey to data-driven hockey improvement.

Unlike more complex solutions, SmartHockey Light focuses on the most important features and provides a streamlined experience that requires no technical expertise whatsoever. You can get started in minutes and immediately begin capturing your first game data.

Key Features

📊 Player Statistics Tracking

Track goals, assists, penalties, and other key performance indicators for all your players. Build a comprehensive statistical picture over the course of the season and identify trends in individual performance over time.

�� Goal Map

Visualize where your team's goals are scored and conceded using an intuitive goal map. Understanding scoring patterns helps you identify strengths and weaknesses in your team's play, enabling targeted improvement.

📅 Season Overview

Monitor your team's performance across the entire season with a clear and comprehensive season overview. Track results, goal differences, and standings. See how your team has improved over time and identify key turning points in the season.

⚡ Quick Data Entry

Enter game data quickly and efficiently with our optimized data entry interface. Record goals, assists, and penalties in seconds, allowing you to focus on coaching rather than administration during and after games.

How SmartHockey Light Works

SmartHockey Light is designed to be as simple as possible while still providing powerful statistics tracking. The app is available as a web application, meaning you can access it directly from your browser without needing to download or install anything.

Getting started takes just a few minutes. Create your team by entering player names, then start recording game data. The interface is clean and intuitive, with large buttons and clear labels that make it easy to find what you need even in the heat of the moment.

During a game, you can quickly log goals and assists by selecting the player and type of statistic. The app updates your statistics in real time, so you always have an up-to-date picture of your team's performance. After the game, you can review the statistics, check the goal map, and export reports if needed.

At the end of the season, the season overview feature gives you a complete picture of your team's performance, showing how you've improved over time and providing data for planning next season. The export function allows you to save all your data and share it with players and club officials.

Web App – Available Now

SmartHockey Light is available right now as a fully functional web application. No download required – just open your browser and start tracking. The web app works on all devices including smartphones, tablets, and computers, so you can access your statistics wherever you are.

The web app is regularly updated with new features and improvements based on user feedback. As a web-based solution, updates happen automatically without you needing to do anything – you always have access to the latest version.

Who Is SmartHockey Light For?

SmartHockey Light is perfect for recreational hockey players who want to start tracking their statistics, small teams and hobby clubs looking for an affordable and easy-to-use solution, youth coaches who want a simple tool without a steep learning curve, players who want to monitor their own development over time, and anyone who is new to hockey statistics and wants to start with the basics before moving to a more comprehensive solution.
