SmartHockey App Comparison

SmartHockey Products offers three distinct applications, each designed for a specific type of hockey player and team. Whether you are an elite coach managing multiple professional teams, a recreational player who wants to track personal statistics, or an inline hockey enthusiast, there is a SmartHockey app designed specifically for your needs.

This comparison guide will help you understand the key differences between SmartHockey Pro, SmartHockey Light, and SmartHockey Inline, so you can make an informed decision about which app is the right choice for you.

Which App Should You Choose?

Choose SmartHockey Pro if...

  • You are a coach or team manager at a club or league level
  • You need advanced analytics and professional reporting
  • You manage multiple teams or work with coaching staff
  • You want a tactical board for planning and game preparation
  • You need professional-quality CSV and PDF exports for club administration
  • You play ice hockey and want the most comprehensive solution available

Choose SmartHockey Light if...

  • You are an individual player or manage a small recreational team
  • You want something simple and easy to use without a steep learning curve
  • You need a free or low-cost solution that gets the job done
  • You want to start tracking statistics immediately without waiting for app downloads
  • You play ice hockey and want the essential features without complexity

Choose SmartHockey Inline if...

  • You play inline hockey or roller hockey
  • You want statistics tracking designed specifically for the inline hockey format
  • You want to track your personal development as an inline hockey player
  • You need line-up management for inline hockey teams
  • You want to access your statistics on any device without downloading an app

Our Recommendation

If you are new to hockey statistics tracking, we recommend starting with SmartHockey Light (for ice hockey) or SmartHockey Inline (for inline hockey). Both are available immediately as web apps and give you a great introduction to hockey statistics tracking without any complexity.

If you are a serious coach or team manager who wants the most powerful features available, SmartHockey Pro will be the right choice when it becomes available. Its advanced analytics, tactical board, and multi-team management capabilities make it the ideal tool for professional-level coaching.

All three apps are developed by SmartHockey Products, a company dedicated to bringing professional-quality analytics tools to the hockey community at every level of the game. We are committed to continuous improvement and regularly add new features based on feedback from our users.

Unsere Empfehlung

Wenn du neu im Hockey-Statistik-Tracking bist, empfehlen wir mit SmartHockey Light (für Eishockey) oder SmartHockey Inline (für Inline-Hockey) zu beginnen. Beide sind sofort als Web-Apps verfügbar und bieten eine hervorragende Einführung in das Hockey-Statistik-Tracking.
