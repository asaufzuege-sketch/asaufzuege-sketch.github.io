The SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro is our flagship application — a comprehensive, browser-based web app designed specifically for ice hockey coaches, assistant coaches, and analysts who demand the highest level of precision in game analysis. Unlike traditional pen-and-paper methods or cumbersome spreadsheet solutions, the Team Tracker Pro puts a full professional statistics suite directly in your hands, accessible from any smartphone, tablet, or laptop right from the bench or the stands.

At the heart of the app is a powerful real-time tracking engine that lets coaches record every meaningful on-ice event as it happens. You can log shots on goal (distinguishing between shots from different zones), goals with full assist attribution, penalties including penalty type and duration, power play and penalty kill situations, faceoffs, blocked shots, hits, giveaways, and turnovers — all with a single tap. The intuitive interface is designed so that even during the heat of a period, data entry never takes more than a second.

Beyond basic event tracking, the Team Tracker Pro calculates advanced metrics automatically. Plus/Minus ratings are updated live for every skater, giving you an instant snapshot of each player's defensive and offensive impact. Corsi and Fenwick statistics — the gold standard for possession-based analysis in modern professional hockey — are computed continuously so you can see shot-attempt differentials at even strength in real time. Ice time (Eiszeit) per player and per line is tracked with precision, helping coaches make informed decisions about deployment and line matchups.

The app supports full roster management for multiple teams and seasons. You can define lines, assign jersey numbers, and track statistics across an entire season. After each match, detailed summary reports can be reviewed directly in the browser and exported. Because all data is stored locally in your browser, no server account is required and your data always remains private and under your full control. The app is available as a Progressive Web App (PWA), installable on Android via Google Play for offline use at the rink.

Inline Tracker особенно популярен среди тренеров, которые одновременно ведут как хоккейные, так и инлайн-хоккейные программы. Как и все приложения SmartHockey Products, Inline Tracker основан на браузере и хранит все данные локально.
