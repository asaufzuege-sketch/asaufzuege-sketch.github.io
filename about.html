About SmartHockey Products

Our Mission, Philosophy & Story

SmartHockey Products was founded with a single, clear conviction: every ice hockey coach — regardless of budget, club size, or technical background — deserves access to professional-grade game analysis tools. For too long, detailed statistical tracking and performance analysis have been the exclusive domain of NHL and KHL franchises with full-time analytics departments and expensive proprietary software. We believe that is wrong, and we set out to change it.

"Great coaching decisions come from great data. We make that data accessible to every coach on the planet, from the smallest junior league rink to the professional arena."

Our Story

SmartHockey Products grew out of a genuine frustration felt by coaches at the amateur and semi-professional level. During games, these coaches were forced to rely on handwritten notes, basic spreadsheets, or expensive systems originally designed for professional teams — none of which were truly fit for purpose in the fast-paced, tablet-on-the-bench environment of a real hockey game.

Our founders — with backgrounds spanning both competitive hockey and software engineering — began building the first version of what would become the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro in response to this exact problem. The goal was radical simplicity on the surface, combined with genuine analytical depth underneath. A tool that could be operated with one hand during a penalty kill, yet produce the kind of Corsi and Fenwick reports that rival those used in the NHL.

Over time, and through close collaboration with active coaches across multiple countries and leagues, the product evolved into a fully featured, multilingual, cross-platform Progressive Web App — available on any device, in the browser, and installable on Android via Google Play.

Our Philosophy

At SmartHockey Products, we are guided by four core principles that shape every feature we build:

  • Accessibility First: Every coach should be able to use our tools, not just those with large budgets or technical teams. Our web-based approach means there is nothing to install, no annual licence fee for core features, and no special hardware required.
  • Real-World Usability: Software built for coaches must work under pressure, on a phone screen, in a noisy arena. We obsess over interface speed, touch-target size, and the number of taps needed to record a critical game event.
  • Data Privacy: We believe your coaching data belongs to you and your club. That is why all statistics in the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro are stored locally in the user's browser — no cloud account, no data sharing, no third-party access.
  • Continuous Improvement: Hockey evolves, analytics evolve, and so do our products. We maintain a close dialogue with coaches around the world and regularly ship new statistics modules, export formats, and quality-of-life improvements based directly on their feedback.

What We Build

Our flagship product, the SmartHockey Team Tracker Pro, is a comprehensive real-time game-tracking application. It captures a full range of statistics including shots on goal, goals with assists, penalty events, power plays, penalty kills, faceoffs, hits, blocked shots, giveaways, and turnovers. Advanced metrics including Plus/Minus, Corsi, and Fenwick ratings are calculated automatically and updated live throughout the game. Ice time per player and per line is tracked with precision.

Beyond the flagship app, SmartHockey Products also offers companion tools for lighter tracking needs and inline hockey management. All products are built with the same philosophy: maximum usability, genuine analytical value, and complete respect for user data privacy.

Our Global Reach

SmartHockey Products tools are used by coaches and teams across Europe, North America, and beyond. Our apps are available in English, German, French, Swedish, Finnish, and Russian — reflecting the truly international nature of the hockey community we serve. We have collaborated with coaches who have worked in the NHL, KHL, Swiss National League (NL), and various European Extraligas to ensure our statistics definitions and workflows match real-world professional standards.
